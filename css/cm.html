COMPUTER HARDWARE ENGINEERING

The technology of today is computerised automation. Every activity involves the use of computers or computing components. So the study of computer hardware has a vital role in the recent and future technology. Click here for Syllabus.

Study-Spectrum of Programme

The various areas of curriculum includes

  • Design of logic systems and computers

  • Operating system and other system software design and case studies

  • Design of Microprocessor and microcontrollers based systems

  • Embedded systems

  • Computer assembling, maintenance and troubleshooting

  • Data communication and computer networks

  • Information Security

  • Programming in various languages like C, C++, python, java etc.

Along with the above the curriculum focuses on the personal development by including the courses on

  • Health and physical education

  • Life skills

  • Environmental science

  • Management skills

Scope of the Programme

There is a big gap between the requirement of diploma level computer engineers and their availability. The curriculum of diploma in Computer Hardware Maintenance fills this gap to some extent. The diploma holders can develop their career in national, multinational and Govt. organisations or they could become entrepreneurs. The major employment opportunities are

  • Hardware Engineers

  • Network Professionals

  • System Administrators

  • Software Developers

  • Training professionals etc.

There are ample opportunities for the diploma holders for higher studies. They include

  • Lateral entry to B. Tech. courses leads to M. Tech. and research

  • Specialisation programmes in software, Networking and Hardware fields leads to International Certifications

  • AMIE etc.
