Government Polytechnic College, Meppadi is one of the major Technical institutions in Wayanad district situated at Thanjilode. GPTC Meppadi carries a creditable record of supplying several technicians, technologists and a group of talented persons to the nation. Government Polytechnic College, Meppadi was established in the year 1999 . Located in a rural area , the scenic beauty and serenity of the surroundings appears most suited for academic pursuits. The institution stands less than 6 kilometers from Meppadi, which is only 12 km away from Kalpetta. At present the institution is in a campus of 10 acres at Thanjilode.

This institution offers three year diploma Programmes in three major disciplines of Engineering / Technology ( Computer Hardware Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electronics Engineering ) under the Directorate of Technical Education, Govt. of Kerala. The institution is approved by the ALL INDIA COUNCIL FOR TECHNICAL EDUCATION, NEW DELHI.
